EAT. BREATHE. SLEEP. FOOD.

Born from a dream and a potato — a story of passion, travel, and the love of bringing people together.

When Alberto announced to his parents that he wanted to be a chef, his father sent him to peel potatoes at a friend’s tavern. His goal was to discourage him. The result was Alberto proving to both himself and others that he was born to be a chef.

Proud Potato Peeler was born as a brand that honours that moment: the point in time when he realised his calling and chose to chase it. Every dish is rooted in that playful mix of grit, warmth, and a touch of nostalgia.

Today, Alberto’s journey has taken him through award-winning kitchens across Europe. Proud Potato Peeler brings those stories back to the table — in a space where friends gather, conversations linger, and every plate is made with a whole lot of heart.
