A Deep-Rooted
Nomad

Most of Proud Potato Peeler's recipes are influenced by his grandmothers who hailed from Turkey and Greece. In his dishes inspired by Levantine cuisine, Alberto can also trace the special imprint of his strong-willed dad, a paterfamilias who regularly cooked for his family when Alberto's mother was busy at work.

The dishes on the menu change according to the seasonality of ingredients (in Singapore it is always summer) and the time of the year (Christmas, Chinese New Year, etc.), so keep your eyes peeled for the updated menu each month.
