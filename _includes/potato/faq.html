Frequently Asked Questions

Here's a little help section — and if you need more, we're only one click away.

Do I need a reservation?

+

For the restaurant and bar, we recommend it — we get pretty popular around meal times! The bakery (when it's up) is drop-by friendly.

Can I visit all three in one day?

+

Absolutely! Many guests love experiencing our restaurant for lunch, enjoying drinks at the bar in the evening, and grabbing fresh pastries from the bakery. It's the perfect day out!

Do you offer takeaway or delivery?

+

Yes! We offer takeaway from all locations. Delivery is available through major food delivery platforms. Check our website for current delivery partners and hours.

Are you family-friendly?

+

We welcome families with open arms! We have kids' menus, high chairs, and a relaxed atmosphere. The bakery is especially popular with little ones.
